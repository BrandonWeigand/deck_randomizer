Deck Randomizer

Instructions

This is a deck shuffiling helper. The program randomly chooses the next pile for you to put your card in.

Deal the cards with your domanate hand while holding the remander of the cards on your other hand.

You must set a time it takes to deal and number of cards in the deck to above zero before you can begin.

The sliding bar represents how long you have before you need to deal the next card

There is a feint after trail to remind you of the previous area that was dealt to.

Pause and Play to opperate
